An Example of our Work

Creating an Online Presence for Fizantakraal Trout Lodge

The Brief

Fizantakraal Trout Lodge wanted to modernise their website and create an online presence that reflected the high standards and luxurious yet natural feel of the lodge itself. The site needed to reflect the experience of Fizantakraal and show potential guests what the Fizantakraal experience is about.

Redesign and Creating Identity

The design of the site reflects the style of the lodge and its surrounds, and is built to harmonise with the images of Fizantakraal to recreate the experience of Fizantakraal online. We built the site to be fully responsive for desktops, small screens, tablets and mobile phones.

Content and Story-telling

We provided copywriting, and worked with Dan at Fizantakraal to decide what information guests needed, and developed an information heirarchy that enabled guests to both get an overall feel for the lodge, and also easily find specific content.
